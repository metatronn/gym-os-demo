# Rita — Skeptical Gym Owner (User Perspective)

## Background
Runs a CrossFit box. Tried Mindbody (too expensive, terrible UX), Wodify (clunky, couldn't customize), and went back to spreadsheets + Square for payments. She's heard "we're building the operating system for gyms" before. She'll look at GYM OS, but she's walking in with crossed arms.

## What She's Thinking
- "Show me the pricing on page 1 or I'm leaving"
- "14-day trial with no credit card? Fine, but if I can't import my 150 members easily, it's already over"
- "The dashboard better not be all fluff — I want to see MY numbers, not demo data"
- "If the billing integration breaks, I lose real money. How do I know Stripe webhook handling actually works?"
- "I don't have a Slack workspace. Do I need one? Is this built for tech companies or for gyms?"
- "What happens to my data if I cancel?"

## Her Dealbreakers
1. Can't see real pricing without talking to sales
2. Onboarding takes more than 15 minutes
3. No way to export data
4. Billing feels unreliable or opaque
5. Requires integrations she doesn't use (Slack, etc.)
6. Mobile experience is an afterthought

## What Would Win Her Over
- Working product in 5 minutes with her real data
- Clear, honest pricing with no hidden fees
- Reliable payment processing she can trust
- A system her part-time desk staff can learn in one shift
- Visible proof that data is private and secure

## When to Consult This Perspective
- When designing the pricing/landing page
- When building the onboarding flow
- When deciding which integrations are required vs optional
- When designing data export/portability
- When writing copy or documentation
- When something feels "enterprise-y" — Rita will bounce
